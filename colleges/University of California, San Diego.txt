--- GENERAL_INFO_START ---
Minimum GPA: California Resident: 2.4 minimum in all UC-transferable courses; Non-Resident: 2.8 minimum in all UC-transferable courses. Highly selective majors and all applicants should strive for a higher GPA.
Application Deadlines: Application Filing Period: November 1 - 30 (for Fall admission).
Required Tests: SAT/ACT scores are not used in the admissions decision.
Transfer Pathways: UCSD does not participate in the Transfer Admission Guarantee (TAG) program.
General Education Certification: Completion of the Intersegmental General Education Transfer Curriculum (IGETC) is recommended. The UC 7-Course Pattern is required for minimum eligibility.
Application Components Required: 4 Personal Insight Questions (PIQs) from 8 prompts are required as part of the UC Application. Letters of Recommendation (LORs) are generally not accepted but may be requested on an individual basis. Portfolio/Interview: Not generally required.
Maximum Transferable Units: A maximum of 70 semester (105 quarter) units of credit for lower-division coursework from any institution is granted. Units from AP/IB/A-level exams are not included in this limitation.
Minimum Transferable Units: 60 semester (90 quarter) UC-transferable units by the end of the spring term prior to fall enrollment (Junior-level standing).
Maximum Pass/No Pass Units: No more than 14 semester (21 quarter) units of the required 60 units may be taken Pass/Not Pass.
