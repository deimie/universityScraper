--- GENERAL_INFO_START ---
Minimum GPA: Competitive applicants have a GPA of at least 2.5/4.0.
Minimum GPA by School: Kogod School of Business: 2.75; School of Education: 2.70; School of International Service (SIS): 3.00; School of Communication: 2.50; School of Public Affairs (SPA): 2.50.
Application Deadlines: Fall Semester Priority Deadline: May 1; Spring Semester Priority Deadline: October 1. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis after the priority deadline.
Minimum Transferable Units: No minimum requirement to apply; however, High School Transcripts are only required if the applicant will have completed less than 24 semester hours of college coursework.
Required Tests: Standardized tests (SAT/ACT) are not required for transfer applicants. English Proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS/PTE Academic/qualifying SAT/ACT scores) is required for international transfer applicants whose native language is not English.
Transfer Pathways: Guaranteed Admissions Partnerships exist with seven community colleges: Howard Community College (MD), Green River Community College (WA), Daytona State College (FL), Miami-Dade College (FL), Montgomery College (MD), Northern Virginia Community College (VA), and Tidewater Community College (VA). Requires a Letter of Intent by October 1 for fall consideration.
General Education Certification: IGETC/CSU Breadth/TAG are not specified as pathways. Transfer students must satisfy AU Core requirements, including Quantitative Literacy I & II, Written Communication and Information Literacy I & II, Habits of Mind, Diversity and Equity, and the Capstone. Students transferring 60+ credits must complete only one Habits of Mind course.
Application Components Required: Common Application, Application Essay (one required via Common Application), $75 Application Fee/Fee Waiver, Official Transcripts from *all* colleges/universities attended, Official High School Transcript (if under 24 semester hours completed), Statement describing activities if out of school for one semester or longer, College Report (completed by a college official), Letter(s) of Recommendation are not required unless specifically requested by your intended academic department or major.
Maximum Transferable Units: Maximum of 60 semester credit hours from an approved two- or four-year institution. A cumulative maximum of 60 credit hours from all sources (college coursework, AP, IB, CLEP, military credit).
Minimum Transfer Grade: Grade of "C" or above in the transfer course.
Residency Requirements: At least 45 of the last 60 credit hours must be completed in residence at American University.
